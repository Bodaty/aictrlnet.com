What is AICtrlNet?

AICtrlNet is an AI orchestration platform that treats humans and AI as equal workflow participants. Build approval flows, review queues, and collaborative AI processes—not just automated pipelines.

Key Capabilities
Human-in-the-Loop Native
Approval nodes, review queues, and escalation paths built into the core architecture.
Multi-Framework Support
LangChain, AutoGPT, CrewAI, AutoGen, Semantic Kernel—use what works for you.
Protocol Support
MCP, A2A, and 27+ adapters for AI services, databases, CRMs, and platform integrations.
Visual + Code
Build workflows visually with the drag-and-drop builder or programmatically via Python SDK and REST API.
AI Governance
Audit trails, bias detection, and explainability reports. Built for regulated industries.
Open Core
MIT-licensed Community Edition. Genuine open source with commercial Business and Enterprise tiers.
